Tutorial

How to use the Daily Food Tracker

1) Add a food entry

  1. Type a Food name (example: “Chicken Burrito”).
  2. Enter Calories as a positive number (example: “650”).
  3. Enter a Food Type (example: “Protein”, “Carbs”, “Fats”).
  4. Click Add to save the entry.

2) View your total calories

After you add foods, the Total Calories number updates automatically by summing all calorie values in your list.

3) Delete an entry

Each food item has a Delete button. Clicking it removes that entry from the list and updates the total calories instantly.

4) Data saves automatically

MacroTrack saves your food list in your browser using localStorage. If you refresh the page, your saved foods should still be there. (Note: clearing browser storage will remove saved entries.)

Tips

  • Calories must be a positive number, or the app will show an error.
  • Use consistent food types (Protein/Carbs/Fats) to keep your log organized.
  • Keep entries short and readable (example: “Oatmeal”, “Greek Yogurt”).
